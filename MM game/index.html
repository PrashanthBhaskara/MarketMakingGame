Four-player sum contract

Market Maker Card Game

Game setup

The deck has one card of each rank from 2 through A. Everyone sees only their own private card and any revealed board cards. The contract settles to the sum of all four private cards plus the three board cards.

Rule assumption used here: play the configured number of markets, reveal one board card, repeat. The game settles immediately after the third board card is revealed.
